Cells TD

Play Game Here!

Description

Protect your body by fighting the bacteria and viruses using your immune system. Cells TD is a tower defense game where you fight off hordes of bacteria and viruses. Use your bodies' immune system such as the red and white blood cells to fight off the enemies in your body.

My Contributions

Cells TD is a group project for a research paper alongside the development of this game. My contribution is I scripted the functionalities of the game.
