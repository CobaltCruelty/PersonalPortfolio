Realm Survivors

Play Game Here!

Description

Realm survivor is a top down roguelike where players enter hostile areas and survive waves of enemies. Players each round get a random set of items that can improve their combat ability in order to survive the onslaught of enemies. Survive through this rounds in order to get cash and purchase other classes that have different playstyles.

My Contributions

Realm survivors is a game that I have developed in a few months as a solo project. Development includes all of the assets in the game including all of the scripting needed to make the game possible. Only the music and soundeffects were not developed by me.
